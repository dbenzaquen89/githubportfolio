More about Me

May 3, 2022 by Daphne

Daphne Benzaquen Creator, Consultant, and Entrepreneur Daphne Benzaquen has gained many new experiences and skills while growing her business since graduating with her MBA from Olin Business School in 2017. During her MBA career Daphne launched sustainable fashion lifestyle brand, daph., a brand that bonds her unique style with her Peruvian roots showcasing Peruvian craftsmanship and sustainable resources through timeless handbags, accessories, and apparel. In 2019 Daphne was named one of St. Louis Business Journal’s ’30 under 30’. Most recently, Daphne has been featured in Women’s Wear Daily, MSN.com, The Skimm’ and was a Fashion Group International Rising Star Finalist in the Accessory and Sustainability categories. From her work in the fashion world and business knowledge, in 2019 Daphne launched Daphne Benzaquen Consulting. The company was born organically since Daphne was already constantly meeting with entrepreneurs, sharing insights, reviewing websites, recommending marketing strategies and consulting on business growth based on her success. Daphne’s focus is centered around coworking spaces, education, therapists, food, and lifestyle brands. She hopes to use her growing Javascript skills to further help her clients. In her free time you can find Daphne cooking different recipes or visiting local eats in her hometown of St. Louis, MO. Food plays such a big part in her life that she co-founded The Chomp to share all her best restaurant recommendations and everyday recipes.
